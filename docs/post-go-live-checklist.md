# Post Go-Live Checklist

## First Hour

- Confirm login, access context, one read-only route per role and no cross-organization visibility.
- Check error rate, authentication failures, RPC latency and alert delivery.
- Reconcile first financial command through outcome, journal and audit correlation.

## First Day

- Reconcile payments, wallets, orders, inventory and courier exceptions against the cutover baseline.
- Review duplicate/replay metrics and all denied sensitive commands.
- Confirm database and Storage backups completed and are readable.

## First Close

- Execute the signed monthly-close checklist, reconcile every control account and retain reviewer evidence.
- Record defects, remediation migrations and approved risk changes.
- Do not exit heightened monitoring until owner and finance approve the first close.
