# Moderator Guide

Moderators work only with assigned customers and orders. They may create/update permitted pre-production records, apply policy-compliant discounts, record payment evidence and request refunds. They cannot confirm payments, read Ledger/payroll/partner data, approve their own request or bypass a denied action.

Check the displayed organization and assigned record before acting. Record clear evidence and report stale-version or permission errors to an administrator rather than retrying with changed data.
