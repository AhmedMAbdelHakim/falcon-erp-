# UAT Guide

Use `phase-3.5-uat-package.md` in authorized staging. Assign named reviewers who do not approve their own sensitive requests. Each case records fixture, expected and actual result, IDs/correlations, screenshots, pass/fail and notes.

Raise defects with severity, reproduction, role, organization, browser and accounting impact. Critical/High defects block sign-off. Retest fixes from a clean fixture and preserve prior failed evidence. UAT completion requires dated owner, finance, operations and technical approval.
