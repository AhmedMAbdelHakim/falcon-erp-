## Contract Rules

- All money remains signed EGP minor-unit `bigint`; no browser-side financial truth is introduced.
- Accounting dates are interpreted in `Africa/Cairo`; list cursors are deterministic compound cursors.
- Financial reports include `generated_at` or `last_posted_at`, period/as-of filters, reconciliation state, role requirement, and drill-down identifiers where applicable.
- Closed periods remain readable and immutable. Open-period adjustments appear in their posting period and retain `affected_closed_period_id` for drill-down.
- Private base tables receive no new direct grant. API wrappers are explicitly granted one function at a time to `authenticated`; `anon` and `PUBLIC` remain revoked.
- Detailed payroll and partner data continue through their existing RLS relations. New dashboard and reconciliation contracts expose aggregates only.
